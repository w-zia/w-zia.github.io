API Examples

Get Wind & Solar Forecast

  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json"

List All Data Sources

curl -X GET "https://api.nztc.com/v1/sources" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json"

Getting Started

Welcome to the NZTC Data Platform. This comprehensive guide will help you get started with accessing and integrating energy sector data.

What is the NZTC Data Platform?

The NZTC Data Platform is a comprehensive data discovery and access platform for the energy transition. It provides:

  • Real-time and historical data across 13 energy departments
  • RESTful APIs for seamless integration
  • High-quality, verified datasets
  • Professional support and documentation

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade my subscription?

You can upgrade your subscription from your dashboard or contact our sales team for enterprise solutions.

What's the difference between real-time and batch data?

Real-time data is updated continuously (every few minutes), while batch data is updated daily, weekly, or monthly depending on the source.
